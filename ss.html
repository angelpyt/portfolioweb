Overview


This is a final project for Web Development Course that searches non-profit organizations across the United States. The results are shown in the table format, as well as locations in map.


  • DATE: Sep 2015
  • CREATED WITH: Parse, firebase, Leaflet JS
  • ROLE: Front-End Development / Web Design

Problem

This was made for people that are interested in volunteering and interested in non-profit organizations information. I was inspired by my personal experience involving with non-progit organizations that often times it is difficult to get right information at one place. This website intended to give users more insight about the non-profit community through displaying their locations around the U.S., displaying their website, and displaying some statistics about them.
